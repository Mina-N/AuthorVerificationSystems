Bangalore International Islamic Institute Campus is in principle a center of madness and celebrates the cultural festival Unmaad every year in style. Thousands of students, including students from Mumbai, Pune and Delhi gathered and participated in traditional activities. The first day, I saw various activities such as literary activities, Indian songs, antakshri, Street play, and the biggest rock music event in town - B - schoolf rock (BSOR). The highlight of the first day is a film production workshop where famous Bollywood actors Booman Irani, Madhavan and Sherman Joshi participated were guests. The stars who were already on campus for the shoot of a movie and now shared candid opinions on various topics, including the famous movie "Slumdog Millionaire". The street play event, Nukkad, allowed intellectuals to talk about smoking and fighting terrorism. Students from Pune College and Delhi were joint winners.
