The Vice Chancellor of the University of Ghana (UG), Legon, Prof. Ernest Aryeetey, has been reappointed for the position as his first tenure expires on 31 July, 2014. According to a release authored by Stella A. Amoah, Director of Public Affairs of the varsity dated 28 March, 2014, the Universitys Council gave the nod for the extension during its meeting held on 27 March, 2014.
